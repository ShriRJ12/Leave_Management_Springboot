About Us

Welcome to our Leave Management System. We are dedicated to providing a seamless experience for managing your leaves efficiently.

Our system allows you to:

  • View your available leaves

    • Check your remaining vacation, sick, and personal leave balances.

  • Request new leaves

    • Submit requests for time off, specifying the dates and type of leave needed.

  • Edit or cancel existing leave requests

    • Modify or withdraw previously submitted leave requests as needed.

  • And much more!
